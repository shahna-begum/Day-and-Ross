Join the Day & Ross team.

We’re growing and adding new opportunities every day. Whether you want to drive with us or help our business grow, there’s a place for you in the Day & Ross family.

More than just a business.

Our people are our greatest strength – and we treat them like family. We believe in taking care of our team and making sure Day & Ross is a great place to work. For over a decade, we’ve been recognized consistently as one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies, and we were named a Top Company for Women to Work for in Transportation in 2018 and 2019. We’re looking forward to celebrating our 70th anniversary in 2020 – and we couldn’t do it without the customers, employees, drivers, and owner operators who helped make it possible.
